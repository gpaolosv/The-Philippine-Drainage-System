Location

Pisay

In Pisay or Philippine Science High School (Main Campus), the notable areas that normally get flooded is the parking lot and the areas around the dorm. We plan on of course, putting drainage systems around those areas to reduce flooding in the school.

Outside Pisay

These drainages will be placed in populated areas near bodies of water since the weather easily affects the rise of water which may experience floods often. Well populated areas with heavy flooding and/or lots of trash will also have these drainages constructed for obvious reasons.
